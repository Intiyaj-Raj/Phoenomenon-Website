WHAT WE OFFER

Extra benefits

Remote work with flexible hours

Work from anywhere and set your own hours. Just stay connected with the team and get the work done. Freedom with responsibility.

Autonomy and ownership in your projects

You own your work here. Make decisions, shape direction, and solve problems the way you think is best. Your projects, your call.

Annual performance reviews with salary revisions

We review your work annually. When you grow and deliver, your salary reflects that. Your growth, your reward. It's that simple.

Workshops and learning playgrounds

Regular time to explore new tools, build skills, and share what you've learned. Growth isn't extra here—it's part of how we work.

Access to IT Loyalty Club

Discounts at cafés, in stores, on various services, and more. Basically, things you'd use anyway, just cheaper. Small perks add up.

Equipment provided as needed

Need better gear to do your best work? Let us know. We'll make sure you have everything you need to deliver. No compromises here.

Corporate English classes

Want to improve your English? We cover the classes. It helps you grow and work better with international clients. Skills that matter.

18 paid vacation days

Take your time off when you need it. Plus an extra day on your birthday, because working on your birthday isn't fun. Recharge well.

7 sick days + 7 personal days off

Sick or dealing with life stuff? Take the time you need. These days are separate from your vacation. Life happens, and we get it.

All public holidays off

Every single one. No "working through the holiday" nonsense here. If it's a public holiday, you're off. Enjoy it without any guilt.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions already
answered

01

What fintech platforms do you specialize in?

We’ve worked on SaaS platforms, banking apps, DeFi exchanges, investment dashboards, and financial planning tools. From B2B products to consumer apps, we design fintech UX/UI with focus on trust, clarity, security, and scalability. We handle both MVPs and enterprise systems.

02

How is UX different in financial services compared to other industries?

UX in finance needs more than good design. It must build trust, reduce confusion, and follow compliance rules. We design smooth flows for onboarding, transactions, and account management so users can make decisions easily and safely.

03

Do you help with fintech MVPs?

Yes, we build fintech MVPs fast without losing quality. We handle UX research, UI design, prototyping, and development. Our MVPs are ready for investors and can easily scale into full fintech products.

04

Can you redesign our existing fintech user experience?

Yes. We start with a UX audit to find issues in your product. Then we improve onboarding, dashboards, navigation, and trust elements. We also make sure design is clean, modern, and ready for compliance needs like KYC/AML.

05

How long does a typical fintech design project take?

Small fintech UX projects take around 3–4 weeks. Bigger platforms or full app redesigns usually take 6–12 weeks. We work in agile sprints and share weekly updates so everything stays clear.

06

How are you different from other fintech design agencies?

We focus on performance, not just visuals. Our fintech design systems are built for scale, security, and usability. Every UI component is made to support real financial actions like transactions, onboarding, and compliance.

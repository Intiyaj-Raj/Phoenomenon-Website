DESIGNING HEALTHCARE FEATURES THAT MATTER

Workflow-driven UX built for
efficiency, data integrity, and
regulatory fit

Below is a snapshot of the feature patterns we design most often,
each mapped to a specific clinical task, tested with real users, and
documented for smooth hand-off.

Patient onboarding & identity verification

Paper-free intake, e-consent, and ID checks that flow straight into the EHR.

Telehealth video & secure messaging

HIPAA-compliant HD calls, chat, and file exchange with auto-generated visit notes.

Appointment scheduling & smart reminders

Provider-aware booking, calendar sync, SMS/email nudges, and wait-list back-fill.

EHR & lab integration

Real-time APIs for orders, results, clinical notes, allergies, and med histories.

Remote patient monitoring

Wearable and IoT streams (SpO₂, BP, ECG) with threshold alerts and trend graphs.

Medication management & e-prescribing

Drug-interaction checks, refill tracking, pharmacy routing, and adherence nudges.

AI-driven symptom triage

Conversational intake that routes to self-care, telehealth, or urgent slots by risk score.

Role-based access & audit trails

Granular permissions for staff, admins, and patients, plus immutable compliance logs.

Billing, insurance & claims automation

Eligibility checks, coded encounters, e-claim submission, and payment reconciliation.

Population health & outcomes analytics

Cohort dashboards, risk stratification, and KPI tracking for quality-of-care programs.

Clinical decision support

Evidence-based prompts, drug-dose calculators, and guideline reminders inside the workflow.

Multilingual & accessibility layers

WCAG-compliant UI, screen-reader support, RTL layouts, and easy language toggles for global reach.

Emergency buttons & escalation

One-tap SOS triggers real-time alerts, location sharing, and priority routing to on-call teams.

Patient education library

Curated articles, videos, and care plans auto-recommended by condition and literacy level.

Regulatory & quality reporting

Built-in MIPS/PCMH metrics, automated report generation, and dashboards for auditors.

Challenges

You're here because you need to visualize your idea, right?

Is your concept feasible?

A mockup prototype lets businesses test product viability and gather feedback before full development. By creating a functional representation, companies can spot design flaws, test usability, and make early improvements, saving time and resources.

Worried about the cost of development?

An MVP prototype helps cut costs by testing and refining key features early, ensuring your product meets user needs and saves time and resources.

Do you need a more convincing way to pitch your product to investors?

A high-fidelity UI prototype goes beyond static screens — it simulates a real, functional product with core interactions, basic logic, and realistic data. This allows investors to experience your idea as a working solution rather than a concept, increasing clarity, confidence, and the likelihood of securing funding.

FAQ

Questions already
answered

01

What is a design prototype?

A design prototype is an early, interactive model that represents a product’s core functionality and user experience before full development. It helps validate design concepts, identify usability issues, and streamline the development process. Whether you’re building a mockup prototype or an MVP prototype, this step ensures your idea is viable and ready for refinement.
A design prototype is an interactive model that helps validate product concepts before full-scale development.

02

Why do I need prototyping?

Prototyping is essential in product prototype design as it allows you to test and validate ideas before investing in full-scale development. A well-executed prototype can attract investors, reduce costly revisions, and improve user engagement. With an MVP prototype, you can efficiently test core features and ensure a market fit before expanding development. A product prototype design helps reduce risk, optimize user experience, and attract investors before committing to costly development.

03

How long does it take to create a design prototype, and how much does it cost?

The duration varies depending on the complexity and fidelity of the prototype. A basic UI prototype can take just a few days, while a detailed UX design prototype with multiple screens and interactions may take several weeks. For projects requiring extensive validation, an MVP prototype can extend into months, ensuring functionality and user experience are optimized before launch. The price of a design prototype depends on factors such as the number of screens, level of detail, and required interactions, which usually start from $3.500. Our prototype agency offers tailored pricing to fit different needs, ensuring a balance between affordability and high-quality execution.

04

What tools do you use for prototyping?

We primarily use Figma for UI design prototype creation, offering a collaborative and efficient design workflow. For interactive elements and prototype web design, we may incorporate additional tools to enhance the prototype’s presentation and usability. We use Figma for UI design prototype creation and may integrate additional tools as needed.

05

Can I use the prototype for investor pitches?

Absolutely! Our UI prototype services ensure that your product is presented in a compelling, interactive format. Investors appreciate tangible demonstrations of product potential, and a well-structured product prototype design significantly increases your chances of securing funding.

06

What happens after the prototype is ready?

Once your UX design prototype is finalized, you have several options: proceed to full development, conduct user testing, or refine the design based on feedback. Our prototype agency can assist in the next steps, including prototype web design, MVP development, and setting up a scalable design system.

07

How to embed a Figma prototype in a website?

To embed a Figma prototype in a website, you can generate an embed link directly from Figma. This allows stakeholders and users to interact with the prototype without leaving the page. Our prototype agency can assist with seamless integration, ensuring your UX design prototype is presented effectively for investor pitches or user testing.

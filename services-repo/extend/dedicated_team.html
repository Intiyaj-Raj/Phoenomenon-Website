FAQ

Questions already
answered

01

How to choose a dedicated development team?

Choosing the right dedicated development team involves evaluating expertise, cultural fit, and project requirements. Our hire dedicated development team service ensures you get the right talent to match your needs, with seamless onboarding and integration.

02

When to hire a dedicated development team?

If your project requires long-term stability, specialized expertise, or scalability, it’s time to hire a dedicated software development team. This model is ideal for companies looking to build robust digital solutions without the burden of in-house hiring.

03

How many developers should be in a development team?

The ideal team size depends on your project scope. A small MVP might require just a few specialists, while a complex enterprise platform may need a full software development dedicated team including developers, testers, and architects.

04

What’s the difference between a dedicated team and outsourcing?

A dedicated development team works exclusively on your project, integrating with your workflows, while outsourcing typically involves third-party teams handling multiple projects simultaneously.

05

What industries benefit from dedicated teams?

Industries like Fintech, SaaS, Healthcare, and EdTech benefit from dedicated development team services due to the need for ongoing development, security, and feature enhancements.

06

Why choose Phenomenon Studio for a dedicated team?

As a top dedicated development team provider, we offer fully customized teams, expert consultancy, and end-to-end development support, ensuring your project is handled by the best talent available.

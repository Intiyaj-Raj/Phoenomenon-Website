OUR TEAM EXTENTION PROCESS

Adding top-notch specialists to your
existing team without disrupting
workflow

Initial assessment

Understanding what roles you need and why

We discuss what’s missing from your current team. Is it frontend capacity? A senior backend developer? DevOps expertise? We define the specific roles, seniority level, and how long you need them.

Key steps:

  • Skill gaps and role requirements
  • Project timeline and deadlines
  • Team size and composition needs
  • Budget and engagement duration

Deliverables

Role specifications

engagement proposal

Candidate screening & selection

Finding people who match your technical requirements

We screen candidates based on your tech stack and team dynamics. You interview shortlisted candidates and make the final decision. No one joins your team without your approval.

Key steps:

  • Technical skill assessment
  • Portfolio and experience review
  • Culture fit evaluation
  • Client interviews and selection

Deliverables

Vetted candidate profiles

interview schedule

Onboarding & integration

Getting new team members up to speed quickly

We handle the administrative setup while you focus on technical onboarding. New team members get access to your tools, repositories, and documentation. They join your standups and start contributing within days, not weeks.

Key steps:

  • Contract and compliance paperwork
  • Tool and repository access setup
  • Codebase and project documentation review
  • Introduction to team processes and workflows

Deliverables

Onboarded team members

access credentials

signed agreements

Integration & workflow setup

Working within your existing processes

Extended team members follow your sprint cycles, attend your meetings, and use your project management tools. They report to your team leads and work during hours that overlap with your core team.

Key steps:

  • Integration into sprint planning and dailies
  • Adoption of your code review and deployment processes
  • Communication through your preferred channels
  • Alignment with your development standards

Deliverables

Integrated workflow

established communication rhythm

Ongoing management

Maintaining performance and addressing issues

We monitor performance, handle administrative tasks, and address any issues that come up. If someone isn’t working out or your needs change, we replace them or adjust the team composition.

Key steps:

  • Performance tracking and feedback
  • Administrative and HR management
  • Team adjustments as needs evolve
  • Continuous process alignment

Deliverables

Regular performance updates

team adjustments as needed

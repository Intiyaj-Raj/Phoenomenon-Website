Challenge briefing

Understanding the technical questions you need answered

We start by identifying what you’re trying to solve. Is this about choosing between architectures? Evaluating third-party services? Planning a complex integration? The workshop agenda is built around your specific blockers.

Key steps:

  • Technical challenges and decision points
  • Current system constraints or requirements
  • Budget and timeline considerations
  • Key stakeholders and decision makers

Deliverables

Workshop agenda

technical questions list

Architecture evaluation

Reviewing options and trade-offs for your tech foundation

We analyze different architectural approaches for your product. Monolith vs microservices, SQL vs NoSQL, serverless vs traditional hosting. Each option gets evaluated based on your scale, budget, team capabilities, and timeline.

Key steps:

  • Tech stack evaluation and comparison
  • Architecture pattern recommendations
  • Scalability and performance assessment
  • Security and compliance requirements

Deliverables

Architecture options document

comparison matrix

Integration planning

Mapping out third-party services and APIs

We identify which external services you’ll need (payment processors, authentication, email, analytics) and how they’ll connect to your system. This includes API limitations, data flow, and potential integration challenges.

Key steps:

  • Required third-party services identification
  • API capabilities and limitations review
  • Data synchronization and workflow mapping
  • Integration complexity assessment

Infrastructure diagram

service requirements list

Implementation breakdown

Structuring the build into phases and tasks

We break the technical work into concrete development tasks, organized by priority and dependencies. This gives you a realistic view of what gets built when, and where the complex work lives.

Key steps:

  • Feature-level technical task breakdown
  • Development phase structuring
  • Dependency mapping and critical path
  • Risk identification for complex modules

Cost modeling

Estimating infrastructure and operational expenses

We calculate what it’ll cost to run your product: hosting, databases, CDN, API usage, monitoring tools. You get projections for launch and at different scale levels so you can budget appropriately.

FAQ

Questions already
answered

01

What is a technical workshop?

A technical workshop is a hands-on session designed to evaluate your product idea, assess its technical feasibility, and create a detailed development roadmap. Led by industry experts, these workshops help identify potential risks, streamline the development process, and ensure a successful product launch. By focusing on technical planning and feasibility analysis, a technical workshop provides the foundation for building a high-quality, efficient, and scalable solution.

02

How can a high tech workshop benefit my project?

A high-tech workshop offers hands-on, expert guidance to help align your business goals with technical needs. Designed to refine your vision, enhance IT architecture planning, and improve resource management, these workshops are perfect for businesses looking to streamline operations and boost efficiency. Get actionable insights and practical strategies to take your technology and business planning to the next level.

03

What is covered in a technical analysis workshop?

Join our technical analysis workshop to dive deep into system architecture, conduct detailed feasibility studies, and discover actionable recommendations for improving performance and scalability. Perfect for developers, engineers, and IT professionals looking to optimize systems for growth and efficiency.

04

How does a technical interview workshop help my team?

Our technical interview workshop helps teams hire the best technical talent by improving interview techniques, refining candidate evaluation methods, and enhancing recruitment strategies. Gain the tools to attract, assess, and secure top tech professionals for your team.

05

What value does a technical resources workshop provide?

A technical resources workshop is designed to help businesses streamline their operations by planning the right infrastructure, choosing the best technology stacks, and optimizing resource allocation. By improving efficiency and reducing costs, these workshops provide valuable strategies for IT planning, tech integration, and resource management.

06

What industries benefit from a technical workshop service?

Our technical workshop service is designed for industries such as SaaS, FinTech, HealthTech, and more. We provide expert insights and solutions tailored to industry-specific challenges, helping businesses optimize performance and overcome common hurdles. Perfect for companies seeking specialized guidance in software development, technology integration, and innovative problem-solving.
